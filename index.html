my path here

1990s–early 2000s

grows up in toledo, ohio. develops personality despite this.

college

discovers writing, rowing, and the invigorating feeling of academic deadlines whooshing by. falls in love with football.

post-grad

moves to detroit to teach high school english and yearbook. learns how to lead classrooms, navigate teenage emotions, and hype students up for college apps.

after teaching

dips into financial services. stumbles into software engineering.

software engineer era

builds dashboards, systems, tools. becomes very good at troubleshooting. and mentoring burns out spectacularly. decides to breathe, reset, and rethink everything.

the break

reconnects with creativity, mentoring, sports, and community. starts feeling like herself again.

october 2025

applies to grad school. gets in. cries happy tears. begins the pivot toward sports administration.

now

balancing grad school, lions fandom, and the thrilling art of figuring out the next dream job in sports operations.

future

nfl or collegiate athletic department: operations, communications, administration, or something equally behind-the-scenes and powerful. also: petting her cat.
