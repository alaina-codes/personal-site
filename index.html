my path here

1990s–early 2000s: grows up in toledo, ohio. develops personality despite this. college: discovers writing, rowing, and the invigorating feeling of academic deadlines whooshing by. post-grad: moves to detroit to teach high school english. learns how to lead classrooms, navigate teenage emotions, and hype students up for college apps. after teaching: dips into financial services. accidentally discovers she loves tech via an internal bootcamp. becomes a software engineer. software engineer era: builds dashboards, systems, tools. becomes Very Good At Troubleshooting. burns out spectacularly. decides to breathe, reset, and rethink everything. the break: reconnects with creativity, mentoring, sports, and community. starts feeling like herself again. 2024–2025: applies to grad school. gets in. cries happy tears. begins the pivot toward professional sports. now: balancing grad school, lions fandom, and the thrilling art of figuring out the next dream job in sports operations. future: nfl or collegiate athletic department — operations, communications, administration, or something equally behind-the-scenes and powerful. also: petting her cat.
